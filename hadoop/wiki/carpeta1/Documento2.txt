Tazeh Kand-e Nosratabad (Persian: تازه كندنصرت اباد, also Romanized as Tāzeh Kand-e Noşratābād; also known as Noşratābād) is a village in Chaman Rural District, Takht-e Soleyman District, Takab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 732, in 156 families.
